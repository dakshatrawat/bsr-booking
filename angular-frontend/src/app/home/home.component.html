About Bhagat Singh Resort

Nestled in the serene embrace of the mountains, Bhagat Singh Resort offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Our resort is designed to provide you with unparalleled comfort while you immerse yourself in the breathtaking natural beauty that surrounds us.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, an adventurous mountain experience, or simply a place to reconnect with nature, our resort provides the perfect setting. Each room is thoughtfully designed to offer comfort and tranquility, with stunning views of the mountain landscape.
